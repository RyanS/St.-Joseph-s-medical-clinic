Services

We have Primary Care physicians who have specialty skills in cardiology, neurology, dermatology, opthalmology, orthopedics, ENT, urology, surgery and pulmonary medicine.

We provide acute dental care.

We have nursing staff, social services and pharmacy services available for our patients
