St. Joseph's Medical Clinic

Hours of operation (No appointment necessary):

  • Tuesday & Thursday 9am to 1pm
  • Wednesday 1pm to 4pm

We have Primary Care physicians who have specialty skills in cardiology, neurology, dermatology, opthalmology, orthopedics, ENT, urology, surgery and pulmonary medicine.

We provide acute dental care.

We have nursing staff, social services and pharmacy services available for our patients

Winner of Pensacola Impact 100 Grant in 2009 allowing us to add on to our building to provide more space to care for our patients.

All-volunteer clinic operating solely on contributions from the community. Donations may be sent to the clinic.
